# Phygital Marketing: Engaging Healthcare Professionals with NFC

**Client:** QIAGEN (Respiratory/Lung Diagnostics)
**Product Interface:** Custom Lung Plushies with Embedded NFC
**Core Objective:** High-Value Lead Capture & Physical Event ROI

---

## Slide 1: The Concept - Bridging Physical & Digital
- **The Problem:** Handing out physical swag at medical conferences generates goodwill but zero trackable ROI. You don't know who took them, where they went, or if it led to sales.
- **The Solution:** "Phygital" Marketing. We've embedded a silent, battery-free NFC tag into the QIAGEN lung plushies. 
- **The Action:** When a doctor, researcher, or distributor taps their smartphone to the plushie, they are instantly transported to a curated, trackable digital experience.

---

## Slide 2: The User Flow (The Tap Experience)
1. **The Physical Interaction:** Doctor taps their smartphone to the lung plushie at the QIAGEN booth or later in their hotel room.
2. **Instant Recognition:** The smartphone reads the NFC tag and instantly opens the default web browser (No app download required).
3. **The Destination:** They land on a frictionless, mobile-optimized QIAGEN-branded digital experience.
4. **The Value Exchange:** They are presented with a highly relevant offer (The "Hook") in exchange for their contact information or engagement.

---

## Slide 3: Possibilities & Digital Destinations
*What happens after they tap? The destination can be changed remotely at any time without altering the physical plushie.*

*   **Lead Capture Forms:** *"Enter your work email to receive our exclusive 2026 Clinical Report on TB Diagnostics."* (Name, Email, Job Title).
*   **Gamification (Conference Specific):** *"Tap the lung to spin the wheel! Win an iPad Pro or exclusive QIAGEN lab gear. Enter your email to claim your prize."* (Massive engagement tool).
*   **Rich Media Delivery:** Instantly play a 60-second video demonstrating how QuantiFERON technology works, followed by a "Talk to Sales" button.
*   **Direct Scheduling:** A Calendly-style booking form to instantly reserve a 15-minute consultation with a QIAGEN respiratory specialist.

---

## Slide 4: Real-Time Analytics (Proving the ROI)
*We track every interaction. This turns a simple giveaway into a powerful data intelligence asset.*

*   **Tap Volume & Timing:** See exactly when the plushies are being engaged with (e.g., massive spikes during the lunch break vs. consistent taps three weeks after the conference).
*   **Geographic Tracking:** Map where the taps are occurring globally. Did the plushies travel back to hospitals in Germany, Japan, or the US?
*   **Device Intelligence:** Identify if the audience leans heavily towards iOS or Android for future app development considerations.
*   **Retargeting Profiles:** Even if they don't fill out the form, our system drops a tracking pixel. QIAGEN can serve targeted LinkedIn ads to anyone who tapped the plushie for the next 30 days.
