Slide 01 — The Concept

Bridging Physical & Digital

Turning passive conference swag into a trackable lead-generation machine

❌ Current State

Handing out physical swag at medical conferences generates goodwill — but zero trackable ROI. You don't know who took them, where they went, or if it ever led to a sale.

✨ Phygital Solution

We embed a silent, battery-free NFC tag into the QIAGEN lung plushies — turning every giveaway into a connected digital asset that can be updated, tracked, and monetised remotely.

📱 The Tap

When a doctor, researcher, or distributor taps their smartphone to the plushie, they are instantly transported to a curated, trackable QIAGEN digital experience — no app download required.

Slide 05 — Why Not Just Link The Website

The Low-Resistance Path

Meeting QIAGEN where they are — and walking them forward with data

💬
"Let's just link to our website."
Understandable — but a homepage has a 75–90% mobile bounce rate. The tap happens at peak curiosity. Landing on a generic homepage wastes that moment, captures zero leads, and fires zero pixels. The plushie becomes a novelty, not an asset.
Phase 0
🔗
The Trojan Horse
Week 1–2 · Free to start
We redirect through our system to their existing website. Zero change for QIAGEN. We silently layer on tap analytics, device tracking & pixel fires. First real data — who's tapping, when, from where.
Outcome: Proof of engagement — no creative needed

📄
One Page, One Offer
Month 1 · $1,500 one-time
A single QIAGEN-branded mobile page. One headline. One offer (e.g. "Download the 2026 TB Report"). One form. Includes form, video, game or web destination — plus 3 months basic CRM free. The bounce data from Phase 0 makes this an easy internal sell.
Outcome: 15–35% of tappers leave an email
